Youth Ministry


The goal of the St. Peter's Youth ministry program is to, "lead teens closer to Christ." We strive to do this by offering exciting, engaging and relevant events that will help teenagers to realize how much Jesus loves them and desires to have a personal relationship with them. Whoever you are and wherever you are in your walk of faith, you have a home at St. Peter's. Be sure to check out our next event and join us, especially if you've never joined us before!

Our youth ministry programs are based off of material from LIFE TEEN, Inc. LIFE TEEN is a Eucharistic based youth ministry program which has become an international movement. LIFE TEEN offers a 4-year curriculum for high school based on the USCCB's high school framework of catechesis and a middle school curriculum to prepare teenagers for the High School curriculum. What makes LIFE TEEN unique is it offers comprehensive Catechesis in an engaging and interactive setting.

If you're interested in being on one of our teams just email Kenn DeMoll.

Appalachia Mission 2013

On July 7-13 our parish sent a group of teens, young adults and adults to do missionary work in Kingwoood, WV by evangelizing about Christ's love through rebuilding homes. Our parish has been committed for over 20+ years to this mission and this year we would like to invite you to check out photos and see what it was like! Check out photos from each of our sites:

General Gallery
Site A Site B Site C Site D Site E

Site A - Parker's Peeps

At this site, the home owner is an asthmatic so we replaced her carpet with a hardwood floor. We also added a drainage ditch behind her house to remove water run off that was causing the house to sink. This team also finished work at three other sites, including painting, more flooring, hanging drywall, insulation, a handrail, and a stormdoor!

Full Photo Album

Site B - Lew & Dick's Crews

This home needed a lot of TLC. We replaced the front porch and added a wheel chair ramp to it. We also added a back porch with a few steps. We re-tarred the roof to prevent the roof from leaking. Her kitchen had a waterfall in it whenever it rained. We replaced the floor around the toilet which was sagging. This was our biggest job site for the mission.

Full Photo Album

Site C - The Wolfpack

This house actually burned down due to a chimney fire and has since been rebuilt. The home owner is elderly and needed some help putting the finishing touches on her rebuilt home. We finished putting siding on her house, hung all the interior moulding and added a few gutters to her house.

Full Photo Album

Site D - Lawrence's Crew

The home owners are in poor health and the wife of the home owner is confined to a wheel chair. Their house had no wheelchair ramp so she had no way to easily get out of her house. We added a 40 ft wheel chair ramp and a concrete pad to allow her to enjoy the outdoors. Despite their money being quite limited they payed for all of our worksite materials.

Full Photo Album

Site E - Todd's Crew

This home owner is elderly and needed help replacing an existing wheel chair ramp. The old ramp was not built to last and needed to be demolished and has been completely replaced.
